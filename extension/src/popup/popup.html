About C2PA

Content Credentials (C2PA) provide cryptographic proof of an image's origin, creator, and edit history. This technology helps combat misinformation and verify authenticity.

Note: Most images don't have C2PA credentials yet. The technology is rapidly being adopted by Adobe, camera manufacturers, and AI platforms.
